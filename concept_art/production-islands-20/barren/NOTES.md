# Barren island concept selections

All boards and sources were generated with OpenAI's built-in image generation
tool. `public/assets/gr1/images/home-island.png` supplied only the top-down
world-scale pixel treatment, light, readability, and coastal handoff. The four
islands are intentionally uninhabited; Cinder Crown and Ashen Hook are volcanic,
while Saltbone Flats and Blackneedle Isle are non-volcanic erosional forms.

## Shared prompt contract

Each board contains two distinct, unlabeled top-down alternatives. The selected
source retains only the recorded alternative as one complete centered
island-composite, including its irregular shallow-water and fragmented-surf
apron, with outer water approaching RGB `[8, 48, 68]` before a magenta
connected-border matte. Sources contain no text, UI, logos, watermark,
buildings, docks, roads, paths, fires, monuments, ruins, people, or boats.

## Selections

| Island | Geology | Selected | Selection reason | Target canvas |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Cinder Crown | Volcanic | Left | The sea-breached caldera reads immediately as a crown and creates a distinctive navigable crater-water topology. | `576 x 576` |
| Ashen Hook | Volcanic | Left | The narrow hooked cliff line and sheltered gray cove give the set a strong asymmetric small-island silhouette. | `448 x 512` |
| Saltbone Flats | Salt/limestone | Left | The manta-ray outline, branching salt pan, and paired chalk ridges are readable without relying on literal bone imagery. | `640 x 448` |
| Blackneedle Isle | Eroded dark stone | Right | The four-lobed coast and central needle cluster differ more strongly from the long winter skerry and keep several shallow channels visible. | `512 x 512` |
