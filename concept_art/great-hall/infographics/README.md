The sheets share the selected refined Ancestor Wall's pixel-art language:
dark woven teal, warm timber, rope, sailcloth, shell, restrained turquoise,
and gold reserved for returned idol knowledge. Exact generation and correction
prompts are retained in [PROMPTS.md](PROMPTS.md).

Count-sensitive finals were checked against the contract: the era rail has 12
shells, its fourth is selected, and the completed navigator plaque has four
closed voyage notches.
