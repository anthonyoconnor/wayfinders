## Shared generation constraints

- One asset per square image; never a collage, contact sheet or sprite sheet.
- Crisp detailed pixel art matching the existing Wayfinders concept art.
- Consistent upper-left light, lower-right shadows, deep navy ocean and turquoise shallows.
- No UI, text, grid, fog, navigation overlays, map markers or player vessel.
- Uninhabited islands contain natural terrain only.
- Inhabited variants contain static settlement architecture but no people, boats, smoke or other changing activity.
- Shoals remain fully submerged and visibly passable, with no exposed land or blocking rocks.
