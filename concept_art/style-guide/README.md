# Wayfinders visual-direction studies

These generated images accompany the
[Wayfinders art style guide](../../docs/Wayfinders_Art_Style_Guide.md). They
preserve explored directions for later agents and artists. They are concept art,
not runtime assets, approved screen specifications, or evidence of gameplay
behavior.

Use them to discuss hierarchy, material language, mood, silhouette, and the
range of flavor the project can support. Do not reproduce a whole image by
default. Current feature contracts determine meaning, and current runtime
assets determine actual scale and integration constraints.

## Cohesion studies

- `example-01-refined-pixel-gameplay.png` explores a cohesive opening scene
  with organic ocean boundaries, a quiet field around the island, and a
  maritime artifact UI.
- `example-02-water-shoal-language.png` studies water depth, broken highlights,
  reefs, currents, and fishing-shoal marks as one related visual family.
- `example-03-great-hall-harmonized.png` studies a calmer, more unified Great
  Hall composition with clearer portrait and voyage hierarchy.
- `example-04-semantic-encounter-kit.png` explores distinct silhouettes for a
  vessel, wreck, survey site, cave, island, and shoal.
- `example-05-island-geology-family.png` explores how volcanic, cay, atoll,
  basalt, ridge, and delta islands can share a family while retaining different
  geology.
- `example-06-atmosphere-overlay.png` explores cloud rhythm, organic fog,
  route-state graphics, and restrained encounter overlays in gameplay context.
- `example-07-portrait-diversity.png` explores a wider range of dignified
  navigator faces, ages, hair, expressions, and clothing within the shared
  maritime material language.

## Alternative flavors

These deliberately push farther from the current runtime finish. They show how
the shared hierarchy and maritime identity can survive a stronger local thesis.

- `alternative-01-inked-chart.png` treats the world as a restrained engraved
  navigator's chart.
- `alternative-02-gouache-storybook.png` explores a softer painted adventure
  with brighter daylight and simplified crafted panels.
- `alternative-03-woven-chronicle.png` treats the sea and interface as one
  stitched and woven record.
- `alternative-04-moonlit-wayfinding.png` explores sparse bioluminescence,
  indigo water, and warm home lights at night.

These are invitations to experiment, not four preset skins.

## Use and limitations

- Review a direction at the smallest real display size and beside current
  runtime assets before deriving production work from it.
- Treat generated lettering, controls, icon details, coast topology, and asset
  proportions as illustrative. They may be inaccurate and do not override live
  text, accessibility, collision, or gameplay contracts.
- Reuse an idea or relationship, not accidental image-generation artifacts.
- Keep concept files under `concept_art`; production code must not load them.
- Refer to specialist Water, Great Hall, and asset-pipeline documents for exact
  mappings, dimensions, palette handoffs, and repository workflow.

## Provenance

The images were generated during the cross-project visual review on
2026-07-18, using current Wayfinders screenshots and assets as composition and
palette references. Runtime captures, source contact sheets, asset inventories,
and diagnostic target-size boards were not duplicated into this collection.
The superseded first island-family variation was also omitted in favor of the
refined `example-05-island-geology-family.png`.
