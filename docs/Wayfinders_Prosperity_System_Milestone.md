# Wayfinders prosperity-system milestone

Status: `PRS-1` through `PRS-2.4` were completed on 2026-07-19.

The completed batch delivered:

- a session-scoped hidden Prosperity score with cumulative, source-keyed values
  applied only when returned knowledge settles at the exact Home dock;
- one shared Supported-water connectivity authority and immutable returned-fact
  routes for surveyed fishing shoals and surveyed `community` islands;
- the selected **Quiet outriggers** concept direction, implemented as bounded
  code-native fishing-skiff and trade-canoe presentation; and
- deterministic endpoint scheduling, periodic-world projection, reduced-motion
  behavior, player-clearance fading, fixed presentation resources, and scale
  acceptance.

`Wayfinders_Technical_Design.md` owns the resulting gameplay, valuation, route,
presentation, accessibility, and performance contracts. `ARCHITECTURE_MAP.md`
owns current authority and dependency direction. The selected studies and
their provenance remain under `concept_art/prosperity-traffic` as reference art
only. `Wayfinders_Roadmap_Archive.md` owns completion evidence and implementation
history.

No Prosperity spending, economy, settlement growth, score display, save-format
change, or additional traffic family is proposed or authorized.
